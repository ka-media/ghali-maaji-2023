Hon. Ghali Maaji Abdulhameed 2023

Game da Hon. Ghali Ma'aji Abdulhameed

Hon Ghali Ma'aji Tsohon ma'aikacin Banki ne wadda ya kwashe tsawon shekaru yana aiki a Bankuna daban-daban cikin fadin kasar nan, har sai da ya kai matakin Manaja kafin yayi ritaya daga aikin Banki, ya fada harkokin siyasa.

Hon Ghali Ma'aji ya fara harkar siyasa bayan barin sa aikin Banki. A shekarar 2007 ya fara aiki a majalisar wakilai ta tarayya, ya rike matsayin mai bayar da shawara na musamman a harkokin siyasa ga mataimakin kakakin majalisa na kasa daga shekarar 2011-2015.

Hon Ghali Ma'aji ya tsaya takarar kujerar majalisar wakilai a shekarar 2015 karkashin tutar Jam'iyyar APC, Allah bai bashi nasara ba.

Hon Ghali Ma'aji ya rike mukamin kwamishinan Ruwa na jihar Bauchi, ya rike ma'aikatar Noma na wani lokaci, ya rike ma'aikatar Kananan hukumomi shima na wani lokaci, sannan ya rike matsayin mai bayar da shawara na musamman ga Maigirma Gwamnan Jihar Bauchi a fannin cigaban matasa, duk daga shekarar 2016-2018.

A shekarar 2019 ya sake tsayawa takara nan ma Allah bai amince masa ba, daga nan ya sake komawa majalisar wakilai a matsayin mai bayar da shawara na musamman a fannin harkokin Gwamnati ga mataimakin kakakin majalisa na kasa.

Hon Ghali Ma'aji ya kawo cigaba da dama a yankin Katagum, ya samawa Matasa da dama ayyukan dogaro da kai, sannan ya tallafawa Mata da Maza ta fannin lafiya.

Hon Ghali Ma'aji mutum ne mai son cigaban al'umma, sannan yana da ilimi sosai akan harkokin majalisa kasancewar ya kwashe sama da shekaru goma sha yana aiki a ciki da wajen Majalisar.

A yanzu Hon Ghali Ma'aji shine zababben Dan takarar Majalisar Wakilai ta tarayya, a karkashin tutar Jam'iyyar APC a zabe mai zuwa na shekarar 2023.
